About


Our Mission

At Vida Nueva Rescue, our mission is to rescue and care for dogs who have been abandoned, abused, or neglected, and find them their forever homes . We are a no-kill rescue shelter, and we specialize in dogs who have a higher probability of being euthanized at other animal shelters, such as large black dogs, older dogs, and special needs dogs.

Our History

Vida Nueva Rescue was founded in May 2015 by Elizabeth San Martin, an animal lover and animal advocate. Throughout her life, she has owned and rescued many animals... horses, turtles, birds, dogs, fish, and even an iguana. After her beloved English Bulldog passed away, she decided to dedicate herself to dog rescue. Since it began operations, Vida Nueva Rescue has saved 17 dogs from high-kill shelters, 6 dogs from abusive situations, and has successfully placed 13 dogs in their forever homes .

Our Adoptions

Before any adoption takes place, we screen the potential adopters and do home visits to make sure all of our dogs will be properly cared for and loved. Currently, we have 10 amazing dogs available for adoption. If you are interested in meeting them, please send us an with your availability for a Meet & Greet.

Donations

Vida Nueva Rescue is a non-profit 501(c)(3) organization and relies 100% on private donations. Monetary contributions of $100 or more are fully tax-deductible. We also assist with estate planning for those who would like to continue supporting the ideals and efforts of Vida Nueva Rescue in the future.

We also accept non-monetary contributions. We are always in need of supplies for our dogs and for our Long Island City site. We welcome the following private and corporate donations:

  • dog food
  • blankets
  • dog toys
  • cleaning supplies
  • paper products

If you are interested in making a donation to Vida Nueva Rescue, please click on our Donation Form.

Donation Form


If you would like to make a contribution, please click on the PayPal button and you will be directed to a secure online payment site. You will not be charged a fee for using PayPal to make a donation to Vida Nueva Rescue.

If you would like to donate supplies for our dogs and/or our Long Island City site, please check the appropriate boxes and we will contact you to schedule a donation pick-up/drop-off.



Volunteers

The volunteers at Vida Nueva Rescue are a very special group of people. We could not accomplish what we do without their tireless dedication and commitment, and we are grateful for them.

The following are volunteer positions at our Long Island City site:

  • dog walkers (morning, afternoon, and evening hours)
  • dogcare (bathing, feeding, kennel cleaning)
  • special needs (administering medication, wound management, physical therapy)

If you are interested in volunteering at Vida Nueva Rescue, please click on our Volunteer Form.
